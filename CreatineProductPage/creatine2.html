    • Description:

    Creatine monohydrate is a popular dietary supplement primarily used to enhance athletic performance and promote muscle growth. It is a naturally occurring compound found in small amounts in certain foods and synthesized in the body from amino acids. When taken as a supplement, creatine monohydrate increases the availability of adenosine triphosphate (ATP), the primary energy carrier in cells, leading to improved strength, endurance, and recovery during high-intensity exercise. It is widely researched and considered safe for most individuals, making it a staple in the fitness community for those looking to maximize their workout potential.
